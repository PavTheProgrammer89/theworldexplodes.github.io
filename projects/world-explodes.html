The World Explodes in 90 Seconds | How to Play

Welcome to "The World Explodes in 90 Seconds"! In this thrilling game, your objective is to find and activate a button before you explode! Here's how to play:

You have just spawned in. What to do?

As soon as you spawn in, you'll see a countdown timer at the top of your screen. You have 90 seconds to find the button and activate it!

Look around the environment for clues and hints that will lead you to the button's location.
Explore different areas, interact with objects, and pay attention to any visual clues that may help you in your search.

Once you find the button, approach it and hold it to activate it.
Be quick, as the timer is ticking down!

If you successfully activate the button before the timer reaches zero, you win the game!

Shortly, you will be teleported to the 'Winners' Place,' where you should search until you find the more challenges sign,
and head towards it. There, you may participate in different challenges in order to win Coins.
Coins are useful to buy features in the Shop and to trade with others!

What happens if I fail to activate the button in time?

If you fail to activate the button within the 90-second time limit, the game will end, though
don't worry; you can always try again!

Feel free to explore the environment, interact with objects, and strategize for your next attempt.
Remember, practice makes perfect!
