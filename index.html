The World Explodes in 90 Seconds | How to Play


Welcome to "The World Explodes in 90 Seconds"! In this thrilling game, your objective is to find and activate a button before it explodes!. Here's how to play:


You have just spawned in. What to do?



As soon as you spawn in, you'll see a countdown timer at the top of your screen. You have 90 seconds to find the button and activate it before time runs out!

Look around the environment for clues and hints that will lead you to the button's location.
Explore different areas, interact with objects, and pay attention to any visual clues that may help you in your search.

Once you find the button, approach it and hold it to activate it.
Be quick, as the timer is ticking down!
